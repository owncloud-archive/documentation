======================
Help and Communication
======================

.. toctree::
   :maxdepth: 2

Mailing lists
-------------
Communicate via mail on our `mailing lists <https://mailman.owncloud.org>`_.

IRC channels
------------
Chat with us on `IRC <http://www.irchelp.org/>`_. All channels are on **irc.freenode.net**

* Setup: **#owncloud**
* Testing: **#owncloud-testing**
* Development: **#owncloud-dev**
* Design: **#owncloud-design**

Community support forum
-----------------------
 Users can ask for help on `ownCloud Central <http://central.owncloud.org/>`_. We strongly recommend using ownCloud Central
 Note: ownCloud Central hosts special FAQ page where each topic corresponds to typical mistakes or frequently occurring issues

Social Media
------------
You can ask over our social media, including in the ownCloud `Google Plus community <https://plus.google.com/+ownclouders/>`_., the `Facebook <http://www.facebook.com/ownclouders/>`_. or on `Twitter <http://www.twitter.com/ownClouders/>`_.


Maintainers
-----------
